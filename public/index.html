Underwhelming stocks

Don’t let the name fool you, underwhelming stocks can actually be what you want to invest in. For example, Starbucks, SBUX is down 18% in 2024 and they are taking a major hit. For a company like Starbucks though you have to look at the big picture. Starbucks is a huge widespread company and has been bringing in profit for many years, so a bad year isn’t a big deal. But the key is when you’re investing is to really take a look at what you are investing in. While Starbucks is a huge company, they have lots of competitors, like Dunkin, and McDonalds. Starbucks’ main product is coffee, the price of coffee at Starbucks is five dollars and fifty cents whereas at a place like Dunkin it’s less than two dollars. So, sometimes underwhelming stocks can be the future. Or, underwhelming stocks can just keep getting worse.
