For a company like Starbucks though you have to look at the big picture. Starbucks is a huge widespread company and has been bringing in profit for many years, so a bad year isn’t a big deal. But the key is when you’re investing is to really take a look at what you are investing in. While Starbucks is a huge company, they have lots of competitors, like Dunkin, and McDonalds.
